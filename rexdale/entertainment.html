Woodbine Racetrack is a must-see in Rexdale. Horseracing is a major part of Rexdale's identity, as evidenced by local streets named Queen's Plate, Harness, Secretariat, Thoroughbred, and Frost King. All year round, standardbred (harness) races are scheduled during the day, while thoroughbred racing is scheduled for the night. Go ahead and place bets, but you don't have to. One of the nuances of horseracing, compared to other sports, is free admission for virtually all races (except for a handful of marquee, usually televised, events).
