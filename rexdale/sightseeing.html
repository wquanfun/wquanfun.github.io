As stated on the Humber Arboretum Web site: "The Humber Arboretum exists to establish and maintain living plant collections, demonstrate correct horticultural and arboricultural techniques, conserve and restore natural areas and wildlife, facilitate research and education, and provide a welcoming space for people to gather."

Runs across Toronto; accessible from Humber College and Esther Lorrie Park in Rexdale
The Humber River Recreational Trail has been a part of the Rexdale landscape for years, but was revitalized and turned into a legacy gift of the 2015 Pan Am Games in Toronto. A pleasant and scenic bike ride, rollerblade, or walk in itself, the Pan Am Path also provides quirky opportunities to try out other physical activities, like rope climbing and chin-ups. If you enjoy your stroll too much, you might find yourself in downtown Toronto.


www.panampath.org
